About ML Strength

Our Philosophy

ML Strength is Brisbane’s newest name in health and fitness. We believe fitness is a way of life, not a fad. Our gyms offer an old-school feel with a new-school attitude, focusing on speed, strength, power and sports therapy.

We are a family-operated business with no telemarketers, no sales consultants and no pressure. Our members do our marketing — because nothing is more powerful than word of mouth.

Expert Trainers

All ML Strength trainers are Les Mills accredited and hold Bachelor Degrees in Human Movements. Their expertise ensures safe, effective and athlete-level training for all fitness levels.

Meet Maurice Lidman

Maurice has over three decades of experience in the boxing scene. A former amateur competitor, he now shares his passion as a coach — and is known to jump into the ring as your sparring partner.

His boxing classes are fun, exciting and highly energised, offering one of the best cardiovascular workouts available.
